Matar-paneer Recipe

Description

mutter paneer is a north indian dish from primary western india.

Ingredients

  • Paneer
  • Vegetables
  • Cashews
  • Spices, herbs and Seasonings
  • Onion and tomato

Steps to make Matar-paneer

  1. Make a gravy of onion, tomato and other ingredient.
  2. After that wait for gravy to cooldown and then grind it.
  3. Next cook mutter in pressure cooker
  4. Now saute the paste for ten minutes and then add mutter and Paneerto it.
  5. Let it cook for five to ten minutes more.
